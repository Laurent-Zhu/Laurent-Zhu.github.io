About Me

I am Yifan Zhu (朱羿帆), a third-year undergraduate student in Software Engineering at Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), Xi'an, China.

My current interests center on multimodal large language models, 3D CAD / B-Rep understanding, code generation, and AI agents. I enjoy building research systems that connect language, geometry, and practical applications.

I am currently involved in research on BrepLLM for boundary-representation understanding and an ongoing project on CAD code generation with LLMs. Alongside research, I completed an industry internship on agentic workflow systems and have received the National Scholarship for two consecutive years.
